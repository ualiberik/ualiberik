### My Projects & Competitions
#### Hardware & Embedded
- **LHS-1 Stratospheric Experiment**. Currently, I am working on the experiment that studies thermal insulation efficiency of lunar regolith in stratospheric near-vacuum conditions which is really important for future lunar habitats. Being approved by iEDU Cubes in Space program, it will fly on the NASA's RB-11 high-altitude balloon. You can find details in [this repository](https://github.com/ualiberik/lhs1-stratospheric-experiment).
- **METEOSAT CubeSat**. My Samruk team and I designed, assembled, and tested CubeSat (10x10x10 cm satellite) for meteorological purposes, winning 3rd place in international "Exploring World of Science" competition. You can find our presentation on [google drive](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1RFvTkdutw4TwDOtMAy0-4la7ydMyaImA/view?usp=sharing).
- **Carbon rocket with electronics** — self-designed and launched, with onboard data-collection electronics. I documented it in [YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/_DTKGVNd7k8).
#### Software Simulations
- **N-body gravity simulation** — I programmed gravity in Unity C# and modeled astronomical processes in it. You can find details in my [YouTube Video](https://youtu.be/286jV8SJmT8).
- **Digital Trees Evolution** — I modeled natural selection in my Unity C# simulation. You can find details in [repository](https://github.com/ualiberik/Digital-Trees-Evolution-Simulation).
#### Samruk Lunar Settlement
Samruk Lunar Settlement is a comprehensive 50-page lunar colonization concept made by me and the Samruk team for NSS Space Settlement Contest 2026 — our biggest project yet. We published it on [zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/20254682). We won 1st place in Aeroo Space Settlement Competition with a preliminary, shorter version of this project: [repository](https://github.com/ualiberik/Samruk_ASSC2025).
